Urbanyx Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Z.axis ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Urbanyx geospatial mapping platform. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our users, particularly regarding the handling of geographic and spatial data vectors. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, process, and secure your information.

1. Information We Collect & Process

We limit data collection strictly to what is required to execute spatial computing tasks and manage user accounts securely.

  • Account Profile Data: We collect and store your email address, your sector of occupation, and your stated purpose for signing up. If you choose to provide it during registration, we also store the name of your organization.
  • User-Uploaded Spatial Data: We process and store the geographic datasets, shapefiles, GeoJSON layers, or rasters that you actively upload to your private account environment to render custom maps.
  • System Telemetry & Access Logs: We collect basic technical data — such as IP addresses, API request volumes, and map interaction metrics — purely to optimize platform performance, monitor server load, and enforce server-side token restrictions.

2. The Optional "Locate Me" Feature

The Urbanyx map canvas includes a voluntary "Locate Me" button to help orient your workspace. When clicked, the platform detects your approximate location using your IP address to adjust the viewport camera.

  • Zero Storage Guarantee: Z.axis does not store, log, or cache your location details derived from this feature. This processing happens instantly within your local session to execute the map movement; the data is never written to our database, never saved in your long-term browser storage, and never transmitted to our servers for tracking purposes.

3. Payment Processing via Paddle Disclaimer

Z.axis does not collect, transmit, or store financial billing data. All monetary transactions are managed externally by our partner, Paddle, acting as our Merchant of Record. When upgrading your account, your credit card details or bank info are submitted directly to Paddle. Z.axis receives only an automated verification token and subscription status confirmation (e.g., "Active - Pro Tier") from Paddle to adjust your Analysis Token balance. Your financial data is governed exclusively by Paddle's privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Mapping Infrastructure (Mapbox)

Urbanyx utilizes infrastructure provided by Mapbox to render base maps and geographic vector tiles. When your browser requests map tiles, technical metadata (such as your IP address and standard web request headers) is processed by Mapbox to deliver the visual map interface efficiently. Mapbox processes this data in strict compliance with its own privacy and security protocols. Z.axis does not share your private uploaded spatial data layers with Mapbox or any other third party.

5. Spatial Data Security & Isolation

Any private geospatial data you upload to Urbanyx is logically isolated within your specific account container. Z.axis personnel cannot view, access, or manipulate your private spatial layers unless you explicitly request technical support and grant written permission to inspect your account environment.

6. Data Deletion Rights & Accounts

You maintain complete control over your uploaded geospatial data and account profile.

  • Data Deletion: You can permanently delete your custom map layers at any time directly through your dashboard interface.
  • Account Deletion: If you wish to permanently close and delete your entire Urbanyx account, you may submit an account deletion request directly from your account settings dashboard. Our team will review and confirm your request within 72 hours of submission.
  • Purge Window: Upon account closure confirmation, all private spatial datasets and profile data will be permanently purged or anonymized from our live servers within 30 days, except where legal, compliance, or financial records require retention by law.
